The Danbarry Dollar Saver - Western Hills in Cincinnati, Ohio closed May 29, 2014. A simple posting on their Facebook page reads:

DANBARRY WESTERN HILLS WOULD LIKE TO THANK ALL OF THEIR PATRONS OVER THE MANY YEARS.

TODAY MAY 29, 2014 WILL BE OUR FINAL DAY OPEN.

ALL FILMS, FOUNTAIN DRINKS, AND POPCORN ARE ONLY $1 EACH TO SHOW OUR APPRECIATION!!!!

The photo to the left is from their Facebook page, and we have added some of the photos that they posted there so that fans will be able to see what the theater looked like while it was in operation. Visit the theater's Photo Gallery page to see the collection of 22 photos.

Moviegoers in the area will need to visit one of the other theaters in the area, including the Rave Cinemas Western Hills 14 for first-run movies, and the Danbarry Dollar Cinemas - Turfway in Florence for second-run movies.
